# Winston Scientific Privacy Policy

_Last updated: April 20, 2026_

Winston Scientific is an offline-first calculator and study app by Shadowfetch.

## Data collection
Winston Scientific does not collect personal data from users.

## Tracking
Winston Scientific does not track users across apps, websites, or services owned by other companies.

## Accounts
Winston Scientific does not require account creation or sign-in.

## Analytics
Winston Scientific does not include third-party analytics SDKs.

## Ads
Winston Scientific does not show third-party advertising.

## Local app behavior
The app stores user activity such as calculation history locally on the device as part of normal app behavior.

## Educational content
The searchable study catalog included in the app is based on open educational resources and attribution information is shown inside the app.
