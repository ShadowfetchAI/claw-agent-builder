# Winston Scientific

Winston Scientific is an offline-first scientific calculator and study companion for iPhone and iPad.

It is built for two kinds of users at the same time:
- students who need a fast, clean calculator they can understand immediately
- advanced users who need graphing, calculus, statistics, algebra, and scientific tools in one app

## What it does
- opens with a focus wheel so users can jump into the right math mode quickly
- supports basic, scientific, graphing, statistics, calculus, and linear algebra workflows
- works offline without requiring the web
- includes a searchable catalog of open educational math and science books
- keeps the interface simple enough for school use while still exposing advanced functions

## Included study catalog
- OpenStax Prealgebra 2e
- OpenStax Elementary Algebra 2e
- OpenStax Intermediate Algebra 2e
- OpenStax College Algebra 2e
- OpenStax Algebra and Trigonometry 2e
- OpenIntro Statistics
- OpenStax College Physics 2e
- OpenStax Chemistry 2e
